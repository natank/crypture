# Brand Name Brainstorming Session

**Document:** Brand Name Selection Process - Phase 2  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Date:** January 11, 2026  
**Based on:** Brand Name Requirements and Business Plan Analysis

---

## Executive Summary

This document captures the brainstorming session for selecting a brand name that aligns with Crypture's strategic positioning as a privacy-first, no-login crypto portfolio tracker. The session follows the structured requirements established in Phase 1, focusing on generating creative name ideas that resonate with our three target segments while maintaining clear competitive differentiation.

---

## 1. Brainstorming Framework

### 1.1 Core Naming Themes
Based on our requirements analysis, we're focusing on four primary themes:

| Theme | Target Segments | Key Concepts | Naming Direction |
|-------|----------------|--------------|------------------|
| **Privacy First** | Privacy-conscious (20%) | Security, control, sovereignty, local | Emphasize data protection and user autonomy |
| **Simplicity First** | Beginners (45%) | Clarity, ease, accessibility, instant | Highlight straightforward, intuitive experience |
| **Trust First** | All segments | Reliability, independence, professionalism | Build confidence and credibility |
| **Intelligence First** | Power users (35%) | Insights, analytics, performance, advanced | Appeal to sophisticated investors |

### 1.2 Naming Approaches
We're exploring three distinct naming strategies:

1. **Descriptive Names** - Clear function + benefit combinations
2. **Abstract Names** - Evocative concepts that suggest our values
3. **Compound Names** - Strategic word combinations

## 8. Next Steps

### 8.1 Immediate Actions
1. **Detailed Trademark Search** - Comprehensive search for top 5 candidates
2. **Domain Acquisition Strategy** - Secure available domains for top candidates
3. **Target Audience Testing** - Test names with representative users from each segment
4. **Stakeholder Review** - Present shortlist to brand selection committee

### 8.2 Evaluation Timeline
- **Week 3:** Complete trademark and domain verification
- **Week 4:** Conduct audience testing and stakeholder review
- **Week 5:** Final selection and brand identity development

### 8.3 Testing Framework
**Audience Testing Approach:**
- Survey 50 users per segment (beginners, privacy-conscious, power users)
- Test memorability, pronunciation, and brand association
- Evaluate emotional response and perceived positioning
- Compare against competitive names

**Stakeholder Review Criteria:**
- Strategic alignment with business objectives
- Long-term brand architecture fit
- Market positioning and differentiation
- Legal and regulatory considerations

## Conclusion

The brainstorming session has produced a comprehensive set of name ideas aligned with our strategic requirements. The shortlist provides strong options that balance privacy positioning, simplicity appeal, and professional credibility while maintaining clear differentiation from existing competitors.

**Crypture** stands out as the optimal choice, offering the best combination of domain availability, competitive differentiation, and cross-segment appeal. The name supports our privacy-first positioning while maintaining the sophistication needed for power users and the accessibility required for beginners.

The next phase will involve detailed verification, audience testing, and stakeholder review to validate this recommendation and prepare for final brand selection.
