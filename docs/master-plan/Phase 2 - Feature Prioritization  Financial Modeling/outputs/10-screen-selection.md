# 10-Screen Selection for Investor Presentation

**Document:** Phase 2 Output - Hybrid Master Plan  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Date:** January 2025

---

## Executive Summary

This document defines the 10 screens selected for Crypture's investor presentation. Each screen was chosen based on its ability to demonstrate core value proposition, competitive differentiation, segment appeal, and visual impact. The selection supports a compelling 5-7 minute demo narrative.

---

## Selection Criteria

Each screen was evaluated against the following criteria:

| Criterion | Weight | Description |
|-----------|--------|-------------|
| **Core Value Demonstration** | 30% | Shows fundamental product value |
| **Competitive Differentiation** | 25% | Highlights what competitors lack |
| **Segment Appeal** | 20% | Appeals to 2+ target segments |
| **Visual Impact** | 15% | Impressive for presentation |
| **Monetization Clarity** | 10% | Shows revenue potential |

## Demo Narrative Script

### Opening (Screen 1: Landing Page)

> "Welcome to Crypture—a privacy-first crypto portfolio tracker. Notice our key differentiator right here: **no account required**. Let me show you what that means..."

### Zero Friction (Screen 2: Add Asset Flow)

> "I can add Bitcoin to my portfolio right now—no signup, no email, no password. Just search, enter quantity, and done. The asset appears instantly."

### Core Value (Screen 3: Portfolio Dashboard)

> "This is the heart of Crypture. My total portfolio value, updated in real-time via CoinGecko. Every asset, current price, 24-hour change—all in one clean view."

### Visual Analytics (Screen 4: Composition Chart)

> "For a visual perspective, here's my portfolio composition. Instantly see allocation percentages—in this case, heavy on Bitcoin, diversified across altcoins."

### Premium Value (Screen 5: Performance Tracking)

> "For our Premium users at $9.99/month, we unlock performance tracking. See exactly how much you've gained or lost over any time period. This is the feature users upgrade for."

### Market Context (Screen 6: Market Overview)

> "Beyond your portfolio, we provide market context. Total market cap, top movers, BTC dominance—everything you need to understand where your portfolio sits in the broader market."

### Engagement (Screen 7: Trending & Discovery)

> "Our trending feed helps users discover new opportunities. See what's moving, explore categories, and quickly add promising coins to your portfolio."

### Engagement Premium (Screen 8: Price Alerts)

> "Premium users can set price alerts. Get notified when Bitcoin hits $100K—without giving us your email. Alerts work through browser notifications."

### AI Differentiator (Screen 9: AI Insights)

> "Our Pro tier at $19.99/month unlocks AI-driven insights. This is where we really differentiate—intelligent analytics that help users make better decisions, all without harvesting their data."

### Privacy Proof (Screen 10: Export/Import)

> "Finally, data portability. Export your entire portfolio to CSV or JSON anytime. Your data is yours—it never leaves your device unless you choose to export it. **That's the Crypture difference.**"
